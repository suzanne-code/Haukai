Haukai Restaurant Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

name

contact information

location

your interactions with us

We collect your personal information in order to:

process contact form requests and reservation bookings

Providing some information is optional.

If you choose not to enter contact details, we'll be unable to process reservation requests and respond to contact form queries.

We keep your information safe by storing this in encrypted files and only allowing authorised staff to access the information.

If you choose not to enter the ability to store cookies in your web browser we'll use analytics to improve the overall visitor experience.

We collect information by asking users directly and by using website cookies. Note that a warning will appear to check that you understand the implications of collecting other information. In this case, analytics are well understood and common case. Website cookies are collected to improve the user experiance on our website and do not include personal information.

We keep your information for 1 year at which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing the information digitally.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at info@haukairestaurant.co.nz, or 09 401 4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri Bay of Islands.
